#INVENTORY MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

##OVERVIEW
The Inventory Management System is a desktop-based application developed using Python, Tkinter, MySQL, and Matplotlib. The project helps manage products, suppliers, employees, categories, and inventory records while providing valuable business insights through an interactive analytics dashboard.

The application is designed to simplify inventory operations and improve stock monitoring through data visualization and efficient record management.

##FEATURES

•Employee Management
- Add, update, search, and delete employee records.
- Maintain employee information in a structured database.

•Supplier Management
- Manage supplier information efficiently.
- Perform CRUD operations on supplier records.

•Category Management
- Create and organize product categories.
- Maintain structured inventory classification.

•Product Management
- Add, update, search, and remove products.
- Track product availability and stock information.

•Inventory Tracking
- Monitor stock levels in real-time.
- Identify low-stock and out-of-stock products.

•Analytics Dashboard
✓ KPI Cards:
  
  - Total Products
  - Total Suppliers
  - Low Stock Products
  - Out of Stock Products
  - Total Inventory Value

✓ Data Visualizations:
  
  - Stock Availability Breakdown
  - Product Status Distribution
